Was ist das WeatherPad?

Related to the current climate change, a lot of dangerous situations are caused by the weather nowadays.

For the early detection of these situations, such as hurricanes or intense rain for example, the "WeatherPad" application has been developed.

It helps to get the most precise information about the future weather development by comparing data of seven different weather services, which often differ in actuality.

The advantage is to get a quick overview of available weather information without having to access every single website of each provider.
